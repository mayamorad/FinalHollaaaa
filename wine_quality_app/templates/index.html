DataVine 12 Mission

What makes a wine great? We trained a machine learning model on physiochemical wine data to predict wine quality — and maybe even discover what makes a glass of red or white so irresistible.

This project combines data visualization, model training, and a bit of vino-fueled curiosity to deliver insights into how wine is scored and why.
